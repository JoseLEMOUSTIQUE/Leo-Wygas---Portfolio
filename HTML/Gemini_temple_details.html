Gemini Temple

Interior view

Plan

Exterior view

I decided to work with Nights of the Zodiac. This was a sentimental choice as I wouldn’t have discovered what I wanted to study without the anime version.

I started by watching the movie again and taking some detailed screenshots to make sure that the mood and the textures I used are aligned with the rest.

I started by trying out multiple ways of doing some Greek pillars. As the movie is based on Greek mythology and is about constellations and the goddess Athena.

I am very happy about this project because I feel like I learned a lot about blender trying to make it as detailed as possible which I know is opposed to making a storyboard that should show the main idea without too much details.

Once the first wall finished with textures, lighting and details, I decided to duplicate it on the three other sides. The Gemini in the movie is an illusionist.

This is why I thought it would be interesting to have the four sides identical even though only the front and the back would have a path linked to it. I had a lot of fun exploiting the array function to get some complex shapes to insert in this model.
